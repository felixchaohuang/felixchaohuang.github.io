About me

I am a tenure-track assistant professor at the School of Computing, Montclair State University.

I received my Ph.D. from the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong in 2021, supervised by Prof. Jianwei Huang.

I was a postdoctoral researcher at the University of California, Davis, from Dec. 2021- Jun. 2024, advised by Prof. Xin Liu.

Research Interests

I am generally interested in research that spans both artificial intelligence/machine learning and game theory.

  • Machine learning algorithm devolopment: split learning, federated learning, online learning
    • C. Huang, et al. ``Convergence analysis of split federated learning on heterogeneous data'', NeurIPS, 2024
    • C. Huang, et al. ``FedUV: Uniformity and Variance for Heterogeneous Federated Learning'', CVPR, 2024
  • Applied machine learning: machine learning applications in agriculture and healthcare
    • C. Huang, et al. ``Infection prediction in swine populations with machine learning'', Scientific Report, 2023
    • C. Huang, et al. ``Predicting antimicrobial resistance of bacterial pathogens using time series analysis'', Frontiers in Microbiology, 2023
  • Game theory for machine learning: game theory, mechanism design
    • C. Huang, et al. ``Incentivizing Participation in SplitFed Learning: Convergence Analysis and Model Versioning'', IEEE ICDCS, 2024
    • C. Huang, et al. ``Duopoly business competition in cross-silo federated learning'', IEEE TNSE, 2023
